Earn & Learn makes teaching kids about money simple and fun. Whether you're managing allowances for one child or several, this app gives your family the tools to build lifelong financial habits.

FOR PARENTS

Manage your family's finances in one place:
• Set weekly or custom allowance amounts for each child
• Add or deduct money with detailed transaction records
• View spending analytics and trends over time
• Set category budgets to teach spending limits
• Approve wish list purchases when kids are ready
• Assign chores and tasks with monetary rewards
• Invite co-parents to share family management

FOR KIDS

Learn money skills hands-on:
• Check your current balance anytime
• Track where your money comes from and goes
• Create wish lists and save toward goals
• See progress toward savings goals
• Complete assigned tasks to earn extra money
• Understand spending vs. saving with visual charts

KEY FEATURES

Real-Time Balance Tracking
Every transaction updates balances instantly. Kids always know exactly how much they have, and parents have complete visibility into family finances.

Wish Lists & Savings Goals
Children can add items they want to save for. The app shows their progress and lets them know when they can afford their goals.

Smart Analytics
Visual charts show income vs. spending, category breakdowns, and monthly comparisons. Help kids understand their money patterns.

Category Budgets
Set spending limits by category. Kids learn to manage money within boundaries, building habits that last a lifetime.

Chores & Tasks
Assign tasks with rewards attached. Children learn the value of earning money through work.

Family Collaboration
Invite both parents to manage the family account. Everyone stays informed about kids' balances and transactions.

Secure & Private
Face ID/Touch ID protection keeps accounts safe. Your family's financial data stays private and secure.

WHY EARN & LEARN?

Teaching kids about money shouldn't require spreadsheets or complicated systems. Earn & Learn provides a simple, intuitive way for families to manage allowances while instilling valuable financial lessons.
